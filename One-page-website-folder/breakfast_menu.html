Breakfast Menu

The Avocado-Toast-platter is served with three slices of Avacado toast. One of the
toasts is served with sliced avacado and fresh mozzarella cheese topped with a split
poached egg and black sesame seeds. The second slice is served with sliced avacado,
and cherry tomatoes, topped with fresh mozzarella cheese and roasted sesame seeds. The
third and final slice is served with cream cheese, a poached egg, sliced cherry tomatoes
that are topped with black pepper, and it is all topped with sliced chives.
